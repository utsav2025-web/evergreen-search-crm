Configure your connection to the CRM and Claude AI

CRM Connection
Enter the base URL of your Evergreen Search instance. This is the URL you use to access the CRM in your browser.
No trailing slash. Example: https://8000-abc123.manus.computer
Authentication
Sign in with your CRM credentials (matt or utsav). Only authorized partners can add companies.
Not signed in
Claude AI Enrichment
Optional: Enter your Anthropic API key to enable AI-powered company data extraction. Claude will analyze the page content and fill in company details automatically. Without this key, the extension will still extract basic metadata from the page.
Get your key at console.anthropic.com. Stored locally in Chrome — never sent to the CRM.
Keyboard Shortcut
Open the extension popup from any tab without clicking the toolbar icon.
Mac: ⌘ Cmd + ⇧ Shift + A    Windows/Linux: Ctrl + Shift + A

To customize, go to chrome://extensions/shortcuts in your browser.
